About Craig

I have been in and out of construction for over 30 years.

At one time, I have been a Gas Safe Registered Engineer, Registered Plumber with CIPHE, Part P Domestic Electrician and small works builder.

Previous careers have been as an accountant and a Web Developer.

For over 15 years, I was on Checkatrade with Small Jobs On Time Same Day Plumbers Ltd, with over 1,050 reviews.

I have come across quality trades people both professionally and for personal use in my home.

I now do the marketing and coordinating for a tribe of trades people, each working independently with their own business, yet working collaboratively and accountably with the rest of the tribe.

I have maintained that building issues occur at the interface of trades, where plumber meets tiler etc. The tribe avoids this as we are just that - one tribe.

I am the first line with enquiries and can guide you to the appropriate trades person.

I am also available to help you if you get 'unreasonable' quotes from others or want the insider's view of what work needs doing and what it is a fair price.

Please not that I just introduce you to the trades person. From that point on, the nature of the interaction is purely between you and them.

I am just passing on homeowners to them. Many are associated with trade bodies or Checkatrade who can assist in their usual way with any unresolved issues that arise.
